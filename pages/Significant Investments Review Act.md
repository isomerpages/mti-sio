<h3><strong>What is the Significant Investments Review Act?</strong></h3><p>The Significant Investments Review Act (SIRA) is an Act to protect the national security interests of Singapore by regulating significant investments in, and control of, critical entities. The Act complements existing sectoral legislation by regulating entities which are not adequately covered under these legislation.</p><h4>Designated Entities</h4><p>The designated entities are published in the Government Gazette. You can search the Government Gazette <a href="https://www.egazette.com.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.
